Website 1: FluidGrids

FluidGrids stands as a paragon of fluid design. Its design effortlessly adapts across devices, thanks to its use of relative units like percentages and REMs. The website avoids fixed widths, ensuring elements resize harmoniously with the viewport. The ingenuity behind FluidGrids is how it ensures a consistent user experience regardless of device or screen size.


Website 2: MediaMasters

MediaMasters showcases the power of media queries. It leverages breakpoint-specific styles, catering to various devices from smartphones to widescreen monitors. The website's use of adaptive images, which change depending on the screen's DPI and size, ensures both performance and visual clarity are maintained.


Website 3: FlexFantastic

As the name suggests, FlexFantastic makes ample use of the Flexbox layout. The site demonstrates the capabilities of Flexbox, from reordering content to adjusting alignment, ensuring a cohesive and intuitive layout on all devices. It's a testament to how modern CSS layout techniques can elevate responsive design.
